About us

On console
since 2013.

Mission Control has been crafting revolutionary interfaces and digital solutions for some of the biggest names in business for over a decade — with one mission throughout: transform how businesses and their customers experience the digital world.

Our approach

Exceptional doesn't happen by accident.

Exceptional digital experiences are engineered — through a careful balance of innovative thinking, technical expertise, and a deep understanding of human behaviour. That philosophy guides every project we fly, whether we're developing cutting-edge mobile applications, designing AI-powered tools, or building solutions that push the boundaries of what's possible.

Our work spans industries and continents, but the thread connecting it all is a commitment to building digital products that don't just meet expectations — they redefine them.

It's also why we're the natural choice for rescue and hardening work. We know exactly where AI-generated code cuts corners, because we've spent a decade building the production-grade systems it's imitating.

Our heritage

From the Iron Works to mission control.

We're proud to call Northampton's vibrant Cultural Quarter home. Our studio sits inside Vulcan Works, a Grade II listed building and a standing testament to Britain's industrial heritage. These walls were once part of the 19th-century Vulcan Iron Works — they echoed with the sounds of industrial innovation, and now they witness the digital revolution we're helping to shape.

There's something poetic about developing tomorrow's digital solutions in a space that once drove the industrial age forward. It's a daily reminder of the transformative power of innovation — and it keeps us pushing boundaries in everything we do.
